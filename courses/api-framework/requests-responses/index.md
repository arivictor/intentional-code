---
title: "Requests and Responses"
description: "Decode and validate input safely, and make every response — success or failure — consistent and predictable."
---

A framework earns its keep at the edges: turning untrusted bytes into validated structs on the way in, and turning Go values and errors into consistent JSON on the way out. This chapter builds binding, validation, and a structured error system so your handlers stay short and your clients always get the same shape back.
