---
title: Delivery Patterns
lede: Patterns for planning, review, policy, and safe promotion of Terraform changes.
---

Terraform delivery is where technical structure meets team process.

It is not enough for HCL to be clean if anyone can apply from a laptop, plans are ephemeral, or policy decisions only happen after a broken change reaches production. Good Terraform delivery makes intent visible before apply, keeps plans reproducible, and enforces guardrails where teams already work.

The patterns here focus on making infrastructure changes reviewable and governable without turning delivery into ceremony for its own sake.
